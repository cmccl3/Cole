Spikey R.I.P.

Spikey was such a great dog and truly embodied the title of "Man's best Friend." He loved sitting there while you threw a ball and then ran and grabbed it because he never really understood the concept of fetch. He will forever live in our hearts, and sometimes on our shoes because we have yet to clean up all of his poop. He was the best dog, except when he had explosive diarreah all over grandma's couch, that was a little naughty of him.
